About Us

We Make every stay feel special.

Welcome to Betcha by Homie House, your home away from home! Founded in 2023, inspired by a longing for comfort and connection post-pandemic, we started with one room and a dream to offer cozy, affordable staycation experiences. Named in honor of our owner’s beloved mother, Betcha embodies warmth and hospitality, values we bring to every guest. Thanks to your trust, we’ve grown into multiple thoughtfully designed spaces, perfect for peaceful retreats, family getaways, or work-and-relax stays. We’re excited to welcome you and make your stay truly memorable!
