Welcome to Betcha by Homie House, your home away from home! Our journey began with a single room, a big dream, and the goal of creating a cozy, affordable staycation experience for travelers and locals alike.

Our journey began a year after the end of pandemic in 2023, inspired by a vision to offer cozy, affordable staycation experiences during a time when people longed for comfort and connection.

Our name, Betcha, holds a special meaning—it’s derived from the name of our owner’s beloved mother, a tribute to her warmth and hospitality, which we strive to bring to every guest who stays with us.

Thanks to the love and trust of our guests, we've grown from one humble room to now managing multiple unique, thoughtfully designed rooms. Each space reflects our commitment to comfort, convenience, and personalized hospitality.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat, a family getaway, or a place to work and relax, we’re here to make your stay memorable. Thank you for being part of our story—we’re excited to welcome you to our growing family!
