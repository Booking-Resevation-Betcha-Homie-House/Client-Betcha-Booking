Privacy and Policy

Check-In & Check-Out

The check-in and check-out times are specified in your reservation confirmation. Please ensure that you adhere to the indicated times to help us maintain a smooth and efficient experience for all guests.

Early check-in or late check-out may be accommodated based on availability and subject to additional charges.

Payment

Payment must be made in full upon check-in. Accepted payment methods include Cash, Gcash or Bank Transfer.

Reservation Fee/Security Deposit

A reservation fee is required to reserve the unit. This is non-refundable in case of cancellation. This will also serve as security deposit. This deposit will be refunded upon check-out, after inspection of the property for damages or violations of the rental agreement.

Rescheduling

Rescheduling requests must be submitted at least 7 days prior to the original rental date. Requests for rescheduling made within 7 days of the original rental date may not be accommodated.
